How I Got Here

After serving in the Army after high school, I attended the University of Washington where I studied business with a focus in marketing. For over five years I helped small to medium companies with their online marketing before deciding to pursue my interest in web development. I studied for a year under the virtual mentorship of a front-end developer at Groupon before enrolling at the University of Seattle Web Development Certificate to further develop my skills.
